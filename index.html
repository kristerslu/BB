Exploring the dialogue between Baltic light and contemporary Belgian interiors through oil, texture, and gold leaf.

"Art is not a decoration, but a window into a quiet memory."

Born in the vibrant artistic landscape of Riga and now practicing in Belgium, Beta Beatrise captures the ethereal quality of Northern light. Her work is characterized by a deep connection to natural textures and the careful application of metal leaf.

Her pieces are currently held in private collections across Europe, bridging the gap between classical painting and modern architectural spaces.

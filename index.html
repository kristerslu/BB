The Connection

Elegance in the Raw

Beta Beatrise explores the connection of raw material and refined elegance. Her art uses multi-layer textures and golden elements to create luxurious abstract paintings to be enjoyed and interpreted by anyone.

Her works are designed to be intuitive; you are invited to rotate the canvas and look at them from different perspectives. By changing the orientation, the light reveals new inspirations and ideas, allowing the art to be a personal reflection for every viewer.

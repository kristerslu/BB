The Philosophy

Elegance in the Raw

Beta Beatrise explores the connection between raw material and refined elegance. Her art uses multi-layered textures and golden elements to create luxurious abstract paintings to be enjoyed and interpreted by anyone.

Her works are designed without a fixed orientation; you are invited to rotate the canvas, finding new inspirations and ideas as the light hits the texture from different angles. It is art that moves with you.
